Popcorn Chicken

Description of Popcorn Chicken

--small bite-size pieces of poultry that have been breaded or battered and fried--are popular with consumers
who like to grab their food on the run.

ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • oil for frying
  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts, cubed

Procedure
